Welcome to the ashok traning

Ashoka, also known as Ashoka the Great, was the third ruler of the Maurya Dynasty and is often considered one of India's most remarkable and influential emperors.

Deeply moved by the horrors of the war, Ashoka converted to Buddhism and embraced the principles of non-violence (ahimsa), compassion, and dharma (righteousness). He dedicated himself to the welfare of his people, implementing policies that promoted social justice, animal welfare, and environmental conservation. Ashoka is particularly noted for his efforts to spread Buddhism both within his empire and beyond its borders, sending missionaries to various regions including Sri Lanka, Central Asia, and Southeast Asia.
